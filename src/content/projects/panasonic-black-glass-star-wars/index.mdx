### About the Project

Known by your amazing movies and animations, it's the first time that the Disney has licensed the Star Wars trademark for a home appliance line in Latin America. The line up products unify the excellency and innovation of Panasonic technology with a unique design, inspired by one of the most emblematic characters of all time: Darth Vader.

To launch such a huge release like that, Panasonic counted on FRN Team to develop a fully animated landing page, capable of transmitting all outstanding Star Wars nostalgic aura.


#### Assignment
Develop and deliver the end-to-end solution.
