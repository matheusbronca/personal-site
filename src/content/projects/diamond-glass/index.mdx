### Intro
Panasonic's Diamond Glass refrigerator has a mirrored finish, interactive LED panel, unique design, Inverter technology, SmartSense² technology that monitors the refrigerator's daily use and saves energy, Climate Control that automatically controls humidity inside the drawer, being the most economical in Brazil with an A+++ rating on the INMETRO energy efficiency table.

### About the Project
After the huge success of the Panasonic's Black Glass Refrigerator, Panasonic decides to surprise their customers with a release of a brand new color: Diamond Glass. 

To make some noise in the Brazilian market, Panasonic had prepare an entire marketing campaign.

To fit their needs, FRN³ just created a fully animated landing page that presents the new color and all the other features and benefits of the product.


### Assignment
Develop and deliver the end-to-end solution.
